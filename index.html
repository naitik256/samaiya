    About

    This app uses MediaPipe BlazePose and FaceMesh to detect your writing posture and face direction. Timer runs only when you are truly focused. Real flip clock UI matches your study rhythm.

    ⚠️ Study Guidelines

    • 1. Face and hands must be in camera frame.
    • 2. Keep 25 cm distance from notebook.
    • 3. Maintain 40–50 cm from screen.

    Privacy Policy

    Camera is used only within your browser for posture tracking. No data is uploaded. Your study time is saved locally using localStorage.
