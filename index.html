    About

    This app uses AI posture detection with MediaPipe BlazePose and FaceMesh to track your focus. Timer runs only when you're in writing posture and facing forward. Flip clock adds motivation and focus.

    ⚠️ Important Study Guidelines

    • 1. Keep your face and hands clearly visible in the camera frame.
    • 2. Maintain 25 cm from notebook for posture + eye health.
    • 3. Keep screens 40–50 cm from your face.

    Privacy Policy

    This app uses your webcam only within your browser. No video is saved or sent. Your study time is saved locally on your device using localStorage.
