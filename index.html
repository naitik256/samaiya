About

This app uses BlazePose and FaceMesh to detect your writing posture and face direction in real-time. The timer runs only when you are actively focused on studying.

⚠️ Important Study Guidelines

  • 1. Place your face and hands clearly in camera frame.
  • 2. Maintain 25 cm (10 inches) distance from book or copy.
  • 3. Keep phone/screen 40–50 cm from face.

Privacy Policy

Your camera is used only in your browser for detection. No videos or photos are saved. Your study time is stored locally using localStorage.
