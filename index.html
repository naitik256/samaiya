    About

    This app uses BlazePose and FaceMesh to detect your writing posture and face direction. Timer runs only when you're truly studying. The flip clock UI helps you stay visually focused and motivated.

    ⚠️ Important Study Guidelines

    • 1. Keep your face and hands clearly visible in the camera frame.
    • 2. Maintain 25 cm (10 in) from your notebook for posture + eye health.
    • 3. Keep screens or phones at 40–50 cm distance from your face.

    Privacy Policy

    This app uses your webcam only inside your browser. No video or personal data is uploaded. Study progress is stored safely in localStorage on your device.
